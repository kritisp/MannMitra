Need Help Now?

If you're in immediate danger, please call emergency services. For urgent mental health support, use the SOS button or contact a helpline below.

Verified Helplines

National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS)

Helpline: 080-46110007. This Bangalore-based institute provides medical advice, counseling, and rehabilitation services.

Tele MANAS

Dial 14416 or 1800-89-14416 (toll-free) for 24x7 mental health support and counseling. The service is available in multiple languages.

KIRAN Helpline

For mental health rehabilitation, you can call 1800-599-0019

AIIMS Delhi Helpline (for autism)

1800-11-7776. This 24/7 toll-free service is for parents seeking information and assistance regarding autistic disorders in children.
