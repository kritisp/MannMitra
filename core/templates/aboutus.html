MannMitra: Pioneering India's Digital Mental Health Revolution

MannMitra is a digital mental health and psychological support system for students, designed to build a healthier future by making help accessible and stigma-free.

Join the Community

Why We Built This

With rising mental health issues affecting 1 in 3 students, many struggles with anxiety, depression, and loneliness go unnoticed. Traditional counseling centers are often underutilized due to stigma and a lack of access in rural areas, leaving a gap where problems can escalate without early detection or intervention.

Core Features

AI Chatbot (First-Aid)

A 24x7, multilingual, and stigma-free first-aid chatbot for instant psychological support.

Daily Journaling & Emotion AI

Track your mood with a daily journal and get a personal sentiment analysis to understand your emotional patterns.

Gamified Screening

Take fun and stigma-free mental health screening tests like PHQ-9 and GAD-7 to get instant coping tips.

Psychoeducational Resources

Access curated videos, articles, and guides in regional languages, designed to improve mental health literacy.

Moderated Peer Forum

A safe and confidential community space where students can share experiences and offer peer support.

Govt.-Integrated Dashboard

An admin dashboard with heatmaps and analytics for proactive government intervention and policy-making.

Impact & Scalability

Government-Integrated

Designed as a govt.-backed ecosystem with pilot programs, CSR funding, and the potential for national rollout.

Measurable Social Impact

Our goal is to achieve 25-30% fewer untreated cases and 15-20% lower dropout rates with AI-driven early detection.

Offline-First Design

Designed for low-connectivity areas, our platform allows users to access resources and journal offline, syncing later.

Future Scope & Roadmap

Our solution is designed for sustainable growth, starting with targeted pilots. The mid-term roadmap includes exploring wearable integration and predictive analytics, leading to a long-term nationwide rollout with AI-powered crisis detection.
