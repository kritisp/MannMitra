# MannMitra

**MannMitra** is a student-first digital mental health and psychological support ecosystem.


## Core Features:

1. AI-Powered Self-Help Tools
- 24×7 chatbot that listens, guides, and screens for issues.
- Journaling & mood tracking to help students monitor emotions.
- Gamified screenings (*PHQ-9, GAD-7*) → fun, engaging, stigma-free.

2. Confidential Counseling Access
- Anonymous booking system to connect with trained counselors.
- Instant helpline for urgent support.
- Private & judgment-free space.

3. Peer & Community Support
- Peer forums for sharing experiences safely.
- Multilingual resource hub with culturally relevant content (regional languages, traditional wellness).
- Builds a support network, reduces isolation.

4. Institutional & Policy Integration
- Smart dashboards for colleges/universities → track wellbeing, spot early risks.
- Policy model: 1 counselor per 50 students, CSR tie-ups for funding rural outreach.
- Mental health literacy as part of curriculum → awareness at an early stage.
